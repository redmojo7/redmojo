Wobbio is officially here.

Wobbio is a modern baby tracking application designed to help parents and
caregivers log, monitor, and understand their baby’s daily activities, health,
and development —all in one simple, intuitive interface.

With its first public release, **Wobbio v0.1.0**, the foundation is set for
reliable, comprehensive tracking—from newborn routines to early growth
milestones.

---

## What Is Wobbio?

<img
  src='/images/posts/wobbio-v010/overview.png'
  alt='Wobbio Overview'
  className='rounded-xl border border-gray-300'
/>

Wobbio is your companion for tracking your baby’s day-to-day life:

- Feeding and lactation
- Diaper changes
- Sleep routines
- Baths
- Weight and height
- Daily journals and weekly reports

Everything is organized clearly so you can focus less on remembering and more on
caring.

## Daily Journal

- Feeding, sleep, diaper changes, baths, and growth
- A chronological view of the entire day
- Ideal for reviewing routines or sharing updates with caregivers

It becomes a living diary of your baby’s journey.
