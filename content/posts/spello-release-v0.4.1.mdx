release, bringing powerful new features to this AI-powered English dictation and

learning experience.

- Personal accounts for individualized learning experiences
- Secure login and authentication process
- Cross-device access to your learning progress
- Privacy-focused data management

## Why Choose Spello v0.4.1?

The latest version of Spello offers an even more comprehensive solution for
improving English spelling, typing, and listening skills. With the addition of
Study Mode, user authentication, and answer management capabilities, Spello

The authentication system ensures your data remains secure and accessible only
to you, while the answer submission and history features allow you to track your
progress over time and focus on areas that need improvement.
